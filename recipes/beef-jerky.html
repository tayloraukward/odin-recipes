Beef Jerky

Description

This beef jerky marinade is a delicious mix between sweet teryaki and spicy barbecue that will be sure to leave you satisfied. The ingredients for the marinade and steps for making are down below. Once the marinade is finished, add all of your beef and let it sit in the refrigerator overnight. Add to the dehydrator for 6-8 hours of until it is at your desired temperature. This jerky is a great snack ready to eat right off the dehydrator.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds top round steak
  • 1 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoons ginger
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Steps

  1. Thinly slice all of the steak into 1/8"-1/4" pieces
  2. Add all of the ingredients into a medium bowl and stir until all of the ingredients are evenly combined and the sugar is dissolved.
  3. Add the meat to the marinade and mix until all pieces are evenly coated. Make sure that all of the meat is submerged in the marinade.
  4. Cover the bowl in plastic wrap and let it sit in the refrigerator for 12 hours or overnight.
  5. Place all of the marinated steak into the dehydrator and follow the instructions on your dehydrator.
