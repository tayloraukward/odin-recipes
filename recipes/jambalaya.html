Taylor's Jambalaya

Description

Inspired by my visit to New Orleans, I set out to make my own version of this great creole classic. This jambalaya recipe is very traditional, tastes great, and is simple to make. Make a pot for your family or bring it to a party with friends and you will surely impress!

Ingredients

  • 1 package (12-14 oz) andouille sausage
  • 2 1/2 pounds chicken thighs
  • 2 large onions
  • 1 cup celery (diced)
  • 1 large green pepper
  • 1 bunch green onions
  • 2-3 tablespoons of your favorite cajun seasoning
  • 1 10 oz can of rotel tomatoes
  • 3-4 cups chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
